Our Journey

A Legacy of Innovation and Dedication.

At Aquamate Engineering Pvt. Ltd., our story began more than a quarter of a century ago with a vision and a mission. Founded by Mr. Milind Chaphekar, a seasoned engineer, our company's journey into the world of plumbing and water management was born out of a genuine passion for excellence. As a qualified graduate engineer, Mr. Chaphekar made a bold decision to embrace the plumbing field as a career, setting the stage for a remarkable legacy.

Our Forte

Plumbing and Water Management Mastery

Our enduring commitment to plumbing and water management has been the cornerstone of our success. Over the years, we've honed our expertise, becoming a trusted name in the industry. Our forte lies in providing end-to-end solutions, covering everything from water supply to drainage. We offer a comprehensive range of products and services that cater to the diverse needs of our clients.
What sets us apart is our unwavering focus on innovation. We've continually sought out cutting-edge products and practices to enhance water usage efficiency. As authorized distributors and service providers for renowned brands like Kirloskar Brothers Limited and Lehry Instrumentation and Valves Pvt Ltd, we bring you the very best the industry has to offer.
